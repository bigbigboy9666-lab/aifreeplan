# Free AI Office Tools 2026: PPT, Mind Maps, Meeting Transcription — What You Actually Get

I spent a week testing every major AI office tool to find out which ones actually give you meaningful free access. Here's what I found.

## The Honest Free Tier Breakdown

**AI Presentation Tools:**

Gamma gives you 400 AI credits on signup — enough for about 10 presentations. Free outputs have a watermark and limited export options. Paid plans start at $10/month.

AiPPT offers 4 free generations per day with a library of 200,000+ free templates. You can upload documents and get instant PPTs. Paid plans around $9.9/month.

Xunfei Zhiwen (讯飞智文) provides new-user free credits for text-to-PPT generation. Strong Chinese language support with auto-generated speaker notes. Membership from ¥99/year.

WPS AI bundles into China's most popular office suite. Basic WPS is free; AI features (writing, PPT generation, spreadsheet processing) require an AI membership at ¥148/year.

**Meeting Transcription & Notes:**

Tongyi Tingwu (通义听悟) from Alibaba gives free users roughly 10 hours of audio transcription per month, with AI-generated meeting summaries, chapter navigation, and speaker summaries. Membership ¥99/year. Chinese speech recognition accuracy is among the best I tested.

Feishu Miaoji (飞书妙记) is built into Feishu's free tier. Records meetings, transcribes to text, generates summaries. Storage and duration limits apply. Enterprise plan from ¥50/user/month.

Notion's free plan gives you unlimited pages and blocks but zero AI. The AI add-on costs an extra $10/month on top of the Plus plan ($10/month).

**Mind Mapping & Diagrams:**

XMind free version handles basic mind maps but has no AI. The AI Copilot requires Pro at $5.99/month.

ProcessOn limits free users to 9 files but supports flowcharts, mind maps, UML diagrams, and org charts. AI features are integrated. Membership ¥199/year.

**Document Processing & Search:**

Kimi supports 200,000-character context — the longest I found — making it ideal for processing long documents. Free tier has daily conversation limits. Membership from ¥59/year.

Metaso (秘塔AI搜索) offers ad-free AI search with academic literature retrieval and document upload analysis. Membership ¥99/year.

Doubao (豆包) from ByteDance provides free basic conversations with daily limits. Handles document summarization and smart Q&A. Pro membership ¥99/year.

## My Recommended Free Stack

For daily office work, this combination covers 80% of needs at zero cost:

- **AiPPT** for presentations (4 free/day)
- **Tongyi Tingwu** for meeting transcription (10 free hours/month)
- **Kimi** for long document processing
- **Metaso** for research and search

If your budget allows one paid tool, WPS AI at ¥148/year is the best value — it covers documents, spreadsheets, and presentations in one subscription.

## Key Takeaways

1. AiPPT is the best free PPT tool — 4 daily generations with no credit card required
2. Tongyi Tingwu dominates free meeting transcription with 10 hours/month
3. Kimi's 200K context window makes it unmatched for long document analysis
4. ProcessOn's 9-file free tier is sufficient for most mind mapping needs

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I use AI-generated presentations commercially?**
Most tools allow commercial use of free-tier output, but Gamma free adds a watermark. For client-facing work, paid plans are recommended.

**How accurate is AI meeting transcription?**
Tongyi Tingwu and Feishu Miaoji both achieve 95%+ accuracy for Chinese. Technical jargon may occasionally be misrecognized, but日常 meetings are fully covered.

**Do free tiers require a credit card?**
None of the tools listed require a credit card for free access.

**Which AI office tool is worth paying for?**
WPS AI at ¥148/year offers the best value — one subscription covers documents, spreadsheets, and presentations across all three scenarios.

**What's the biggest limitation of free tiers?**
Usage quotas (AiPPT: 4/day, Gamma: 400 total, Tingwu: 10 hours/month) and export format restrictions. Fine for light use, heavy users should upgrade.
