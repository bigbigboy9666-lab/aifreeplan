Udio is a top-tier AI music generator that creates studio-quality songs from text prompts. Free users get a limited number of generations per day. Paid plans unlock longer tracks, commercial rights, and priority processing.

Udio Free Credits Details

  • Amount: Approx. 10 songs
  • Reset: Resets daily
  • Watermark: No
  • Commercial: Paid only

Udio Key Features

  • Text to Music
  • Multiple genres
  • Vocal synthesis
  • Instrumental mode
  • Song extension

Pros

  • Among the highest quality AI music generators
  • Daily free credits enough for casual use
  • Wide genre coverage
  • Natural-sounding vocals

Cons

  • Free tracks capped at 30 seconds
  • Queue wait during peak hours
  • Chinese song support is limited
  • Paid plan starts at $10/month

Tips

  • Test different genres with free credits before paying
  • English lyrics produce better results than Chinese
  • Instrumental mode needs no lyrics
