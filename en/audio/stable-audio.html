Stable Audio Free Credits Details

Stable Audio is Stability AI's music and sound effects generator. Free tier gives you 20 tracks per month, up to 2 minutes each. Paid plans unlock commercial use and longer generations. Quality is top-tier among AI music tools.

Stable Audio Free Credits Details

  • Amount: 20 tracks
  • Reset: Resets monthly
  • Watermark: No
  • Commercial: Paid plans only

Stable Audio Key Features

  • Text to Music
  • Text to Sound Effects
  • Up to 3 minutes (paid)
  • 44.1kHz stereo output
  • Adjustable style and tempo
  • Commercial license (paid)

Pros

  • 20 free tracks/month on free tier
  • High generation quality with good audio fidelity
  • Supports sound effects, not just music
  • Browser-based, no installation needed
  • Backed by Stability AI's technical expertise

Cons

  • Free tier does not include commercial rights

  • Free tier limited to 2-minute tracks

Tips
