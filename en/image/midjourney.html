Midjourney

★★★★★ No free tier Paid from: $10/month

Visit Official Site →

The most popular AI image generator, consistently setting quality and aesthetics standards. Limited free trial credits, but output quality is worth paying for. V7 is stunning.

Midjourney Key Features

  • Text to Image
  • Image to Image
  • Image Variations
  • Inpainting
  • Style Transfer
  • Ultra High Resolution
  • Standalone Web Editor
  • Discord Bot

Pros

  • Industry-best image quality
  • Diverse artistic styles
  • V6.1 has dramatically improved detail
  • Standalone web editor, no Discord needed
  • Active community with many tutorials

Cons

  • No free tier, minimum $10/month
  • Free trials are rare and unpredictable
  • Generation speed depends on plan tier
  • Requires prompt engineering skills

Tips

  • Try Flux (free) first if budget is tight, quality is also great
  • Use --style raw to reduce Midjourney's style interference
  • Basic plan at $10/month is enough for personal use
