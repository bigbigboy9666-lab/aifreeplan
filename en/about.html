About AIFreePlan

AIFreePlan is an aggregation platform focused on AI tool free credits. The idea is simple: there are more and more AI tools out there, and each one says it has a "free tier" — but how much can you actually use for free? How many credits do you get on signup? Is there a daily limit? Does the free version add a watermark? This information is scattered across individual pricing pages, and it's a pain to track down.

We compile all of this into one place so you can compare without having to sign up for every tool yourself.

What We Care About

We care about one thing: free credits. Not feature lists, not technical specs — just "how much can I use without paying." That's the most practical question.

  • How many credits/tokens you get on signup
  • Daily or monthly usage limits
  • Whether the free tier has watermarks
  • Whether the free tier allows commercial use
  • How often credits reset
  • Hidden paywalls or gotchas

We continuously track these details. AI tool free tiers change fast — what's unlimited today might be paywalled tomorrow. We try to sync these changes as quickly as possible.

Inclusion Criteria

We don't list tools that are purely paid. If a tool has no way to use it for free, it won't appear on AIFreePlan. A tool must meet at least one of these criteria:

  • Has a permanent free tier or free plan
  • Grants usable credits on new account signup
  • Runs a limited-time free promotion or trial
  • Available for free through specific channels (education, open-source programs)

Data Sources

Our data comes from several channels:

  • Official pricing pages and documentation
  • Our own hands-on testing after signing up
  • Community feedback via GitHub Issues
  • Official social media announcements from tool makers

If you spot inaccurate or outdated credit info, please let us know through GitHub Issues. We'll verify and fix it as soon as possible.

Contact Us

Questions, suggestions, or partnership inquiries? Reach us through:

  • GitHub Issues: Submit feedback or suggestions
  • Data corrections: Open an issue tagged "data-correction" or "new-tool"

📌 AIFreePlan is an independent project, not affiliated with any AI tool vendor. Our goal is to provide objective, accurate, and timely free credit information to help users make better choices.
