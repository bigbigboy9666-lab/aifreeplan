Alexa

★★★★☆ No free tier Paid from: Device Purchase

Visit official site →

Amazon's voice-controlled AI assistant that manages smart devices, answers questions, and plays music in your home.

Key Features

  • Voice control for smart home devices
  • Answers questions about weather, news, etc.
  • Sets alarms, timers, and reminders
  • Plays music, audiobooks, and podcasts
  • Makes calls and sends messages

Pros

  • Extremely convenient, hands-free operation
  • Seamlessly integrates with the Amazon ecosystem
  • Expands capabilities with thousands of Skills

Cons

  • Always listening raises privacy concerns
  • Understanding of non-English languages can be inconsistent

Tips

  • Explore the Skills Store regularly to discover useful features
  • Create custom routines for frequently used commands
