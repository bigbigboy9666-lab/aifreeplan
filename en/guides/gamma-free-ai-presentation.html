Gamma AI Presentation Free Guide: 400 Credits to Generate Professional Slides

Gamma is an AI-powered presentation tool that auto-generates slides from a text prompt. Enter a topic, and Gamma handles the layout, images, and content — no design skills needed. The free plan gives you ~400 credits with no credit card required.

)

Gamma Free Plan: What You Get

The free plan details:

  • ~400 credits (one-time grant, does not reset)
  • Gamma watermark on all slides
  • PDF export supported
  • Shareable links
  • No credit card required

    • Key limitation: The 400 credits are one-time only. They don't refresh daily or monthly. You can generate roughly 3-5 complete presentations (10-15 pages each) before running out.

    Paid plans:

  • Plus — $10/month: More credits, no watermark
  • Pro — $20/month: Unlimited credits, custom branding, premium templates

    • If you need more than a few presentations, you'll need to upgrade. But for a quick test or one-off project, the free plan works well.

    What Gamma Can Do

    Gamma supports three output formats:

    Presentation: Auto-generated slides with animations and transitions

    Document: Long-form content with professional layout

    Webpage: Single-page websites you can publish

    Use cases:

  • Work reports and quarterly reviews
  • Product launches and pitch decks
  • Teaching materials and courseware
  • Project proposals
  • Portfolio pages

Gamma vs Traditional Presentation Tools

PowerPoint / Keynote: Most powerful but time-consuming — manual layout can take hours

Google Slides: Free but limited templates and design options

Canva: Many templates but weaker AI generation

Gamma: Fastest AI generation, but free credits are limited

Gamma's sweet spot: when you need a good-looking presentation fast and don't need pixel-perfect control.

How to Use Gamma

1. Go to gamma.app and create an account

2. Select "Presentation"

3. Enter a detailed topic description

4. Choose page count (8-15 pages recommended)

5. Pick a visual style

6. Wait 30-60 seconds for generation

7. Preview and edit individual slides as needed

The more detailed your prompt, the better the output. Don't write "company annual report" — write "2025 annual revenue report for an internet company, focusing on Q3 growth data, audience is investors."

Gamma's Limitations

Watermark: Every page has a Gamma watermark on the free plan. Not ideal for formal presentations.

Credits don't refresh: 400 credits and done. Heavy users will burn through this quickly.

Limited templates: Free plan has fewer template options than paid.

No PPTX export: Free plan exports to PDF only. If you need to edit in PowerPoint, you need the paid plan.

Common Questions

FAQ

How many free credits does Gamma give?

About 400 credits as a one-time grant. Does not reset. Enough for 3-5 complete presentations.

Does Gamma free plan have a watermark?

Yes. Every page has a Gamma watermark on the free plan. Paid plans remove it.

Can Gamma export to PowerPoint format?

Free plan exports to PDF only. PPTX export requires a paid plan.

Gamma vs PowerPoint: which should I use?

Gamma for speed (30-second drafts), PowerPoint for precision. Choose Gamma if you're short on time and design skills.

Does Gamma support Chinese?

Yes. Enter your topic in Chinese and Gamma will generate Chinese-language presentations.

What happens when Gamma free credits run out?

You need to upgrade to Plus ($10/mo) or Pro ($20/mo) to generate new presentations.
