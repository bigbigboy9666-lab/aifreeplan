Home / Guides / Doubao Pricing 2026
  • Doubao now charges 68 CNY/month for Pro, with three tiers: Free, Pro, and Premium
  • Free tier still works for basic chat and writing, but high-performance mode, long documents, and deep reasoning are paywalled
  • Seedance 2.0 video generation remains free on the basic tier
  • At 68 CNY/month, Doubao is mid-range among Chinese AI tools — Kimi costs ~59 CNY, Tongyi Qianwen ~49 CNY
  • Best free alternatives: DeepSeek (free, open source), Kimi (long context), Tongyi Qianwen (Alibaba ecosystem)

    • Doubao finally started charging

    Doubao — ByteDance's AI chatbot with 345 million monthly active users — has officially moved to a paid model. The free tier hasn't been removed, but the features that actually matter for power users are now behind a paywall.

    This was inevitable. Running an LLM for hundreds of millions of users costs real money on GPU compute. Every additional user adds to that bill. ByteDance subsidized Doubao for over two years; the subsidy era is over.

    Here's what changed and whether you should care.

    The three pricing tiers

    Free tier

    • Basic chat, Q&A, writing assistance
    • Basic image understanding and generation
    • Seedance 2.0 video generation (still free, promoted on the homepage)
    • Standard response speed
    • Base Yunque model

    Pro tier (68 CNY/month)

    • High-performance mode with stronger reasoning
    • Longer context window for document processing
    • Deep reasoning for math, logic, and code
    • Priority response during peak hours
    • Higher daily usage limits

    Premium tier (200 CNY/month)

    • All Pro features
    • Even higher usage limits
    • Enterprise feature preview
    • Early access to new features

    There are also Business and Enterprise plans for teams, priced per user.

    Is the free tier still good enough?

    Depends on what you do with it.

    If you use Doubao for casual chat, quick questions, social media captions, or short translations — the free tier is fine. The base Yunque model handles everyday tasks without issues.

    Where it falls short:

    • Long documents: context window is limited, responses get "cut off"
    • Code generation and debugging: noticeable gap vs. Pro
    • Complex multi-step reasoning: the free model tends to "confidently make things up" (actual user complaint from Sogou WeChat search)
    • Peak hours: queuing is common

    The best way to think about it: free vs. Pro isn't "broken vs. working." It's "standard mode vs. performance mode." The question is whether performance mode is worth 68 CNY to you.

    Is 68 CNY/month worth it?

    Compare what else you get for the same money:

    • Doubao Pro: 68 CNY/month
    • Kimi membership: ~59 CNY/month
    • Tongyi Qianwen Pro: ~49 CNY/month
    • ChatGPT Plus: $20/month (~145 CNY)
    • Claude Pro: $20/month (~145 CNY)

    Among Chinese AI tools, Doubao's pricing is mid-to-upper. The advantage is ecosystem integration — Douyin, Toutiao, and Feishu are all connecting to Doubao. If you're already in ByteDance's ecosystem, the convenience factor justifies the price.

    But if you're comparing raw model capability per dollar, DeepSeek's API is dramatically cheaper, and Kimi offers longer context for less money.

    What this means for the AI industry

    Doubao's pricing move is part of a larger trend. In May 2026, "the end of free AI" became a hot topic on Chinese social media. The pattern playing out across the industry:

    Free and good → tiered pricing → quiet feature cuts → usage caps → credit-based monetization

    Gemini free tier is shrinking. Claude free users face rate limits. Now Doubao charges. The "subsidize users to build market share" phase is ending. What comes next is "charge for quality."

    For regular users: basic features will stay free. For power users: good AI is getting more expensive.

    Best free alternatives to Doubao

    For chat and writing:

    • DeepSeek: Completely free, strong model, open source
    • Tongyi Qianwen: Free base tier, Alibaba ecosystem
    • Kimi: Free tier with long context, great for reading and summarizing

    For video generation:

    • Kling AI: 66 free credits per day
    • Hailuo AI: 300 credits on signup
    • Doubao's own Seedance 2.0 (still free)

    For code:

    • Kimi K2.6: Free coding agent, open source
    • GitHub Copilot free tier: 2,000 completions per month
    • Cline: Open source, bring your own API key

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Q1: Did Doubao remove the free tier? A: No. The free tier still exists and works for basic chat, writing, and image understanding. What changed is that advanced features (deep reasoning, long documents, priority speed) are now paid only.

    Q2: How does Doubao Pro compare to ChatGPT Plus? A: ChatGPT Plus ($20/month) gives you GPT-4o access, which is generally stronger in reasoning and code. Doubao Pro (68 CNY/month, ~$9.40) is cheaper but with a weaker base model. If you need top-tier reasoning, ChatGPT Plus offers more per dollar.

    Q3: Is Seedance 2.0 still free? A: As of May 2026, yes. Seedance 2.0 video generation is available on the free tier and is actively promoted on Doubao's homepage.

    Q4: What's the best free alternative if I don't want to pay? A: DeepSeek for general AI tasks (completely free, open source), Kimi for long document processing (free tier with generous context), and Tongyi Qianwen for Alibaba ecosystem integration.

    Q5: Will other Chinese AI tools start charging too? A: Most likely. The trend across the industry is toward monetization. If you find a good free tool, use it while it lasts. DeepSeek's open-source model is the safest bet for long-term free access since anyone can self-host it.

    ❓ FAQ

    Will Doubao's free tier be removed?

    Not for now. ByteDance's strategy is free basics + paid premium. The 345M MAU is their biggest asset.

    Is Seedance 2.0 video generation still free?

    As of May 2026, Doubao's homepage still promotes free Seedance 2.0 access. But how long this lasts is uncertain.

    Doubao Pro vs ChatGPT Plus - which is better?

    For Chinese chat and writing, Doubao Pro understands Chinese better. For English, code, and complex reasoning, ChatGPT Plus is stronger. Doubao Pro costs half as much.

    Which version should enterprise users choose?

    If your team uses Feishu (Lark), Doubao Enterprise offers the best integration. Otherwise consider Qwen Enterprise or ChatGPT Team.

    Will quality improve after Doubao starts charging?

    This is the biggest uncertainty. Wait 1-2 months for real user reviews before deciding to pay.

    Will other Chinese AI tools start charging too?

    Most likely. Kimi, Qwen, and Wenxin are all testing paid models. The free AI window is closing.
