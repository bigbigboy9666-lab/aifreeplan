Last week's article covered 12 platforms. Readers asked for more. So I dug deeper and found 11 additional platforms — some better, some worse, all worth knowing about.

Tier 1: Genuinely Free, Fast, and Feature-Rich

DmxAPI — Most Free Models, Zero Cost

Over 20 free models including glm-5-free, MiniMax-M2.7-free, and kimi-k2.6-free. Email registration only. Pay 1 yuan to activate all free models with no token limits.

Catch: ~5 RPM rate limit. Heavy users report 429 errors during peak hours. Light models perform better; large models may overload.

SiliconFlow — Best Chinese Open-Source Selection

22+ free models including DeepSeek-R1-0528 and Qwen3-8B. Phone number registration, 16 yuan verification bonus. 1,000 RPM per model.

Strength: One key accesses multiple providers. The most comprehensive Chinese open-source model collection available.

ModelScope — Best Multimodal Support

Alibaba's research arm offers 40+ free models: DeepSeek-V4, Qwen3-235B-A22B, GLM-5.1. All models share 2,000 free daily calls, with ~500 per individual model.

Best for: Image recognition, chart analysis, multimodal tasks.

Tier 2: Reliable with Clear Credit Limits

Zhipu AI — Most Stable Domestic Option

8 free models (glm-4v-flash, glm-4.7-flash). Phone registration. No daily call limits, but concurrency restrictions apply.

Why it's reliable: Consistent uptime, predictable rate limits, well-documented API.

GitHub Models — Most Developer-Friendly

13+ free models: gpt-4.1-mini, gpt-4.1, gpt-4o. GitHub account required — no credit card. 15 RPM / 150 RPD.

Key advantage: Access GPT-4o for free with zero friction.

NVIDIA NIM — Fastest GPU Inference

80+ free models: Llama-4-Maverick, DeepSeek-V4, Gemma-3. NVIDIA account required.

Trade-off: RPM ~1-5. Large models take 10+ minutes to respond. Image generation models are fast.

Mistral AI — Generous French AI

Register and receive approximately $25/month in free credits. Supports Mistral Large and other latest models. Le Chat web interface is free.

Caveat: Official site may require VPN in some regions. Data used for model training.

Cloudflare Workers AI — Global CDN + AI

50+ open-source models (Llama-3.1, DeepSeek-7B, Qwen2.5). Free plan includes额度 limits. Serverless GPU inference.

Best for: Projects needing both global CDN acceleration and AI capabilities.

AIHubMix — Best for Code Models

20+ free code models: coding-glm-5.1-free, coding-minimax-m2.7-free. Pay $1 to activate 1 million tokens per model per day.

Standout: coding-glm-5.1 delivers the best coding results among free options.

Qiniu Cloud — Storage + AI Bundle

Free models: glm-4.5-air-free, longcat-flash-lite. New users get 3 million token resource pack. Tested at ~10 million tokens daily.

Unique value: Cloud storage and AI in one platform.

302.AI — New Platform, Limited-Time Free

35+ lightweight free models. Requires $1充值. Fast performance but limited free model capabilities.

CherryIn — New Platform Promotion

25+ free models. Requires $10充值. 450 requests per 30 minutes. Fast response times.

CodePlan X-AIO — AI Coding at Scale

GLM-5.1, Kimi-K2.6, Qwen3.6 models available. One-day trial with 50 credits every 4 hours. Credits accumulate.

Ideal for: Low-frequency coding tasks needing high-quality models.

OpenRouter — Global Routing Platform

DXNT — Smart Routing

55+ models. The dxnt/free endpoint automatically routes to the optimal free model. 100 free daily calls. API keys auto-rotate every 10 days.

100+ models. Generous signup credits. 30 requests per minute. Supports OpenAI, Claude, and Gemini output formats.

Rate Limits and Gotchas

Every "free" API has rate limits. 429 errors during peak hours are normal. Implement exponential backoff.

OpenRouter free tier: Only 50 requests/day.充值 $10 for 1,000/day.

AIHubMix requires $1充值. Without it, only 10 calls/day. With充值, unlimited within model quotas.

Third-party aggregators are less stable than official APIs. For production, use official endpoints.

Platforms discontinuing free models: Tencent Hunyuan hunyuan-lite, LongCat legacy models (May 29).

Mistral AI may require VPN. Access varies by region.
