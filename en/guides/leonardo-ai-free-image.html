Quick Answer

Leonardo AI gives you 150 free tokens daily — enough for roughly 15 standard images. No watermarks on free tier output. Supports Alchemy HD engine and ControlNet for precise control. One of the most generous free AI image generators available.

Leonardo AI offers the best free experience overall: most daily credits, no watermark, rich model selection. Midjourney has better raw quality but zero free credits. Ideogram excels at text rendering but only 5 images/day.

Alchemy HD Engine

Alchemy is Leonardo's quality enhancement engine, available on the free tier. What it does:

  • Richer detail and texture
  • More accurate colors
  • Better composition
  • Higher output consistency

Trade-off: consumes more tokens. Strategy: use standard mode for drafts, switch to Alchemy for final versions.

Community Model Library

Leonardo AI has an active community model library where users upload fine-tuned models. You get access to specialized models for:

  • Photorealistic portraits
  • Anime style
  • Concept art
  • Product design
  • Architectural rendering

Free tier users can access all community models. Many match official model quality — this is Leonardo's unique advantage.

ControlNet Support

Free tier supports ControlNet for precise control over:

  • Human pose
  • Edge detection (Canny)
  • Depth information
  • Semantic segmentation

For designers, ControlNet is essential. Midjourney and DALL-E don't offer this level of control.

3 Common Pitfalls for Beginners

  • 150 daily tokens sound like a lot, but they're not: Leonardo AI's token cost varies by model and resolution. HD generations consume more tokens. Test with low quality first
  • Free tier has watermarks: Leonardo AI free tier images have watermarks. For watermark-free images, try Stable Diffusion (open source free)
  • Model choice affects quality: Leonardo AI has multiple models with big quality differences. Start with the default model, then experiment once satisfied

❓ FAQ

Does Leonardo AI free tier have watermarks?

No. Free tier output has no watermarks — a major advantage over many competitors.

How many images can I generate with 150 daily tokens?

~15 images at standard resolution (512x512), ~6 at HD (1024x1024).

Can I use Leonardo AI images commercially?

Not on the free tier. Commercial use requires the paid plan ($12/month+).

Which is better: Leonardo AI or Midjourney?

Quality: Midjourney wins. Free credits: Leonardo wins. Control: Leonardo wins (ControlNet). Beginner-friendly: Leonardo wins.

Do I need a VPN for Leonardo AI?

Yes, if you're in mainland China. Leonardo AI is not available there directly.
