Google Veo Free: Try Top-Tier AI Video via Gemini, Free Tier Tested

Published 2026-05-24 AIFreePlan
Google VeoGeminiFree VideoAI VideoVideo Generation2026

Google Veo is free to try through Gemini, but the free tier is very limited. Here's what you actually get, how it compares to Kling and Hailuo, and whether the paid plans are worth it.

Quick Answer

You can access Google Veo for free through Gemini (gemini.google.com), but expect only 2-3 generations per day at 720p with watermarks. For serious use, plans start at $7.99/month. The image quality is top-tier, but the free experience is more of a demo than a usable tool.

How to Try Veo for Free

)

Open Gemini, sign in with your Google account, and type something like "generate a video of a cat playing piano." Free tier uses Veo 2 with these limits:

  • About 2-3 generations per day (Google doesn't publish exact numbers)
  • Maximum 5-8 seconds per clip
  • 720p resolution
  • Watermarked
  • Slow generation, often queued

Compared to Kling's 66 daily credits or Hailuo's ~100, Veo's free tier feels stingy. It's a trial, not a real free plan.

Are the Paid Plans Worth It

Google offers four tiers:

Google AI Plus ($7.99/month): More generations, 720p, priority queue. Best value for most users.

Google AI Pro ($19.99/month): 1080p, higher quota, all AI features. Good for content creators.

Google AI Ultra ($249.99/month): Maximum quality and quota. Only makes sense for studios.

For most users, the $7.99 AI Plus is enough. But here's the thing — Kling's $8/month plan gives you way more value. Same price, more credits, comparable quality.

How Good Is the Quality

Veo 3's quality is genuinely top-tier. Physics simulation (water, fire, smoke) is more realistic than Kling or Hailuo. Camera movements feel natural, not floaty.

The catch: free tier uses Veo 2, which is noticeably worse than Veo 3. To experience the full quality, you need at least the $19.99 Pro plan.

How to Choose: Veo vs Kling vs Hailuo

If you're in China:

  • Kling: 66 free credits/day, fast access, 1080p — top pick
  • Hailuo: ~100 free credits/day, no watermark — best value
  • PixVerse: 60 credits/day + 90 signup — best templates

If you're outside China and willing to pay:

  • Google Veo: Best quality, starts at $7.99/month
  • Runway: Professional tools, starts at $12/month
  • Sora: OpenAI's option, wait for general availability

Free users: Go with Hailuo or Kling. Paid users: Choose based on whether you need Google's quality edge.
