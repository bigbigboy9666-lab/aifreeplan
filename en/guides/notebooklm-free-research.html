One-Sentence Summary

NotebookLM is an AI research tool from Google, completely free. Each notebook can hold up to 50 sources (PDFs, webpages, YouTube videos, etc.). The AI helps you read, summarize, generate podcasts, and create video narrations. It's the most underrated free AI tool of 2026.

---

What is NotebookLM

NotebookLM is an AI notebook tool made by Google. You put your materials into it (PDFs, webpage links, YouTube videos, Google Docs, etc.), and it helps you read, understand, summarize, and generate content.

Launched in 2023, it received major updates in 2025-2026. Current features include:

  • Document analysis and Q&A
  • Automatic podcast generation (Audio Overview)
  • Automatic video narration generation (Video Overview, a new feature in March 2026)
  • Automatic report generation (Reports feature)
  • Mind map generation
  • Multi-source cross-referencing

    • The key point: completely free. No subscription required, no credit card needed. Just use a Google account.

    What's Included in the Free Version

    The free version of NotebookLM is the full version—there is currently no paid plan.

    Free quota:

  • Up to 50 sources per notebook
  • Up to 500,000 words per single source
  • No limit on the number of notebooks
  • Unlimited podcast generation
  • Unlimited video narration generation
  • Unlimited report generation

    • No watermarks, no duration limits, no resolution limits.

    ---

    Core Features in Detail

    Document Analysis

    Upload PDFs, paste webpage links, or import YouTube videos, and NotebookLM will automatically extract the content. You can then ask it any question about these materials, and it will answer based on the original text without making things up.

    For example, if you upload 3 papers and ask, "What are the differences in research methods between these three papers?", it will compare them one by one, citing specific paragraphs.

    Podcast Generation (Audio Overview)

    This is NotebookLM's most popular feature. With one click, turn your materials into a podcast of two people conversing.

    The generated podcast isn't a robot reading a script; it's two AI hosts discussing your materials, with questions, answers, and interactions. It sounds very natural.

    Use cases:

  • Turn papers into podcasts to listen to during your commute
  • Turn technical documents into dialogues for easier understanding
  • Turn meeting notes into summary podcasts

    • Video Narration (Video Overview)

    A new feature in March 2026. Similar to the podcast, but it generates a video-format narration. Suitable for visual learners.

    Report Generation (Reports)

    A new feature added in 2026. Generate a complete report from raw materials in one step, including structure, citations, and summaries.

    Practical Use Cases

    Academic Research: Upload 10 papers, and let NotebookLM help you find commonalities, compare methodologies, and generate a literature review outline.

    Learning a New Field: Find 10 articles on a topic, import them all into NotebookLM, and let it help you quickly understand that field.

    Product Research: Import competitors' official websites, review articles, and user feedback, and let the AI help you conduct competitive analysis.

    Reading Notes: Import the PDF of an e-book, and let the AI help you create chapter summaries and extract key points.

    Meeting Notes: Import the text transcribed from a meeting recording, and let the AI help you extract action items and summarize decisions.

    How is it Different from ChatGPT

    ChatGPT is a general-purpose AI assistant, while NotebookLM is a specialized research tool.

    ChatGPT's strengths:

  • More versatile, can chat about anything
  • Rich plugin ecosystem
  • Can generate code, images, etc.

    • NotebookLM's strengths:

  • Answers based on your materials, doesn't make things up (RAG technology)
  • Unique podcast generation feature
  • Completely free, no quota limits
  • Integrated with Google ecosystem (Docs, Drive, etc.)

    • Best combination: Use NotebookLM for research and material organization, use ChatGPT for creativity and generation.

FAQ

Is NotebookLM really completely free?

Currently completely free, no paid plan. But Google may introduce a paid tier (Google One integration) in the future.

Does NotebookLM support Chinese materials?

Yes, Chinese PDFs and web pages work for analysis. But podcast generation is English only for now.

NotebookLM vs ChatGPT, what's the difference?

ChatGPT is general AI, NotebookLM is a specialized research tool. NotebookLM answers based on your sources, doesn't hallucinate.

How to use NotebookLM's podcast feature?

After uploading sources, click Audio Overview to auto-generate a two-host podcast discussion. English only currently.

Do I need VPN for NotebookLM?

Requires Google account. May need VPN in China.

How much can NotebookLM handle?

Up to 500K characters per source, up to 50 sources per notebook.
