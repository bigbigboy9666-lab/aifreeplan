Free AI PPT Tools Compared: Gamma vs AiPPT vs Meitu AI PPT vs ChatPPT

Quick Verdict

Choose Gamma if you want polished presentation design with the most generous free tier (50 per month). Choose AiPPT if you're a Chinese-language user who needs native PPTX export and great CJK support. Choose Meitu AI PPT if you need fast drafts and don't care about fine design details. Choose ChatPPT if you're on a tight budget and prefer conversational editing.

Key data: Gamma gives the most free generations (50/month), AiPPT gives 10, Meitu and ChatPPT give 5 each.

Tool Breakdown

Gamma

Free tier: 50 presentations per month Paid: $10/month (unlimited)

Features:

  • Text-to-PPT, upload documents or paste content
  • 30+ professional templates with auto-layout
  • Image/video/code block embedding
  • Real-time collaboration
  • Export to PDF/PNG/share link
  • AI-powered color and font pairing

Best for: Frequent presentation makers (students, salespeople, lecturers) who want design quality without manual tweaking, and English-content teams.

Drawbacks: Mediocre Chinese layout, free tier has watermark, templates lean toward Western business style, generation is slow (1-2 minutes).

AiPPT

Free tier: 10 presentations per month Paid: ¥99/month (unlimited)

Features:

  • Text, outline, and mind-map to PPT
  • 50+ Chinese-optimized templates
  • One-click color/font swap
  • Local image upload
  • PPTX export (editable in PowerPoint)
  • Team collaboration

Best for: Chinese users (especially domestic enterprises and students) who need editable PPTX output and localized templates.

Drawbacks: Limited free tier (10/month), occasional layout issues, relatively expensive paid plan, free tier lacks premium templates.

Meitu AI PPT

Free tier: 5 presentations per month Paid: ¥29.9/month (unlimited)

Features:

  • One-sentence PPT generation
  • 100+ built-in templates
  • Auto image and color matching
  • Theme switching
  • PDF/image export
  • Mobile app support

Best for: Users who need quick drafts without caring about design details, internal sharing, and mobile users.

Drawbacks: Smallest free tier (5/month), inconsistent quality with occasional layout chaos, only exports PDF/images (no PPTX), limited template variety and customization.

ChatPPT

Free tier: 5 presentations per month Paid: ¥19.9/month (unlimited)

Features:

  • Conversational PPT generation
  • Page-by-page editing and adjustment
  • 40+ templates
  • Image upload
  • PPTX export
  • Real-time preview

Best for: Users who prefer interactive editing, page-by-page fine-tuning, and budget-conscious individuals.

Drawbacks: Small free tier (5/month), slow generation, fewer templates, mediocre Chinese understanding, basic features even on paid plan.

Practical Advice

If you're a student: Use Gamma. Most free credits, solid design quality, great for course presentations or thesis defenses.

If you're an office worker: Use AiPPT. Best Chinese support, generates PPTX files you can edit in PowerPoint, professional for internal sharing.

If you're in a hurry: Use Meitu AI PPT. Quality is average but it's the fastest generator, good for emergencies.

If you're on a tight budget: Gamma's free tier is enough — no need to pay.
