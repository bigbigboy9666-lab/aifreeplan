Advanced Tips for Pro Users (Boost Your Results by 200%)

Just knowing how to use it is basic; using it well is mastery. Here are a few tips:

1. Keywords Should Be Like Ordering Off a Menu: Don’t just say “rock.” Try “Hard Rock, Distorted Guitars, Powerful Male Vocals, Fast Tempo, 90s Style.” The more detailed the keyword breakdown, the more precisely the AI draws from its “music material library.”

2. Lyric Structure Is the Soul: AI is smart – it can recognize tags like `[Verse]` (主歌), `[Chorus]` (副歌), `[Bridge]` (桥段). Using these helps structure the song naturally. For example:

`[Verse]` I see the city lights blur...

`[Chorus]` But I’m chasing the sunrise...

3. Use “Remix” for Targeted Tweaks: Like the melody but not the lyrics? Use Remix. Want to change the genre entirely? Keep the lyrics and switch to “Jazz, Smoky Club, Smooth Male Vocals.” This saves time and lets you refine specific elements.

4. Iterate Relentlessly: Don’t settle for the first result. Generate 3–5 versions. Treat each batch as an exploration – you’ll eventually find one that surprises you.
