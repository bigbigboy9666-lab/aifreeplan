DeepSider: Browser Extension, Install and Use

What Is It

DeepSider is a browser sidebar extension for Chrome and Edge. Once installed, you can access AI models directly from any webpage, including Google Gemini.

Free Tier

  • 30 queries per day (resets at UTC 00:00)
  • Supports all basic models: GPT-4o-mini, Claude 3 Haiku, Gemini 1.5 Flash, etc.
  • No credit card needed, just sign up
  • No VPN needed

How to Install

  1. Visit deepsider.ai
  2. Click "Get Extension"
  3. Choose Chrome or Edge
  4. Login with Google or GitHub

Supported Models (Free)

  • OpenAI: GPT-4o-mini, GPT-3.5-turbo
  • Anthropic: Claude 3 Haiku
  • Google: Gemini 1.5 Flash, Gemini 1.0 Pro
  • Meta: Llama 3.1 (8B, 70B, 405B)
  • Mistral: Mistral-7B, Mixtral-8x7B

Who Is It For

  • Quick research and Q&A
  • Trying multiple AI models
  • Users who want easy access without VPN

Other Free Channels

Besides DeepSider, there are other ways to use Gemini for free:

  1. Google Gemini Official - gemini.google.com, has free tier (VPN required)
  2. Google AI Studio - aistudio.google.com, official lab (VPN required)
  3. Antigravity IDE - Google's new coding tool with Gemini 3.0 Pro (VPN required)
  4. LMArena - Model testing platform, free (VPN required)
  5. ZenMux - Chinese aggregation platform, free

Which One to Choose?

Need Recommended Tool
Daily Q&A, no VPN DeepSider
Coding, have VPN Antigravity IDE
Try multiple models DeepSider
Learning AI Google AI Studio

Final Thoughts

DeepSider is currently the best option for users in China who want to use Gemini without VPN. Other channels mostly require VPN access.

I recommend installing DeepSider first - it's likely enough for most needs.
