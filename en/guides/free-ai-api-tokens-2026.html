Best Free AI API Platforms in China

Moonshot Kimi — Best for Long Documents

Kimi's standout feature is its massive context window. Feed it a 50-page research report or a legal contract, and it handles it effortlessly.

How to get credits: Register at platform.moonshot.cn, complete personal or enterprise verification. You'll receive 15 yuan in credits, valid for approximately 3 months.

Best for: Research summaries, legal document review, long-form analysis

Zhipu AI (BigModel) — Most Generous Signup Bonus

Zhipu gives away an absurd amount of free tokens. Register and immediately receive 20 million tokens — split across GLM-4.6v (6M), GLM-4.5-air (12M), and general-purpose tokens (2M). Valid for 3 months.

How to get credits: Sign up at bigmodel.cn. Click "Finance" then "Resource Pack" in the top right to view your balance. API keys are auto-created on the homepage.

Math: At 1,000-3,000 tokens per conversation, 20 million tokens equals tens of thousands of chats.

Tencent Cloud — Longest Expiry Period

Complete real-name verification and receive 1 million tokens for the Hunyuan large model plus 1 million tokens for the embedding model. Valid for one year — the longest expiry of any platform.

How to get credits: Search "Tencent Hunyuan API" on cloud.tencent.com, click "Activate Now." Find your resources under "Resource Management" on the left sidebar.

Best for: Slow-and-steady projects where you don't want credits expiring on you.

iFlytek Spark — Quick and Easy

A well-established AI platform with strong math and general Q&A capabilities. Register, click "Spark Model" on the homepage, and claim 5 million tokens. Supports Spark X2, Qwen3, DeepSeek V3, and more.

Note: No明确 expiry period announced — use these credits promptly.

Alibaba Cloud Bailian — Most Models Per Account

Think of Bailian as a model supermarket. Register + real-name verification, create an API key, and check "Model Usage" for your credits.

Key detail: Each model gets 1 million tokens separately, valid for 90 days once activated. Models include GLM-5, DeepSeek-V3.2, wanx-v1, Qwen3.5-Omni-Plus, Qwen3-TTS-vd, and gte-rerank-v2.

One account can accumulate dozens of models' worth of credits — the most generous multi-model offering available.

SiliconFlow — One Key, Many Models

A smart aggregator that lets you call DeepSeek, GLM, Qwen, and others with a single API key. Register with real-name verification and receive a 14 yuan voucher, valid for 6 months.

Perfect for developers who want to test multiple models without managing separate accounts.

MiniMax — Voice and Text Versatile

Strong in voice synthesis and text generation with multimodal capabilities. New users receive a 15 yuan voucher, valid for 2 months. Useful for comparison testing or as a backup endpoint.

AI Ping — Lightweight Trial

Register for 1 yuan, complete real-name verification for another 5 yuan. Supports MiniMax-M2.7, GLM-5-Turbo and other mainstream models. Best for quick, lightweight experiments.

Best Free AI API Platforms Internationally

GitHub Models — No Credit Card Required

Use your GitHub account to access GPT-5 for free. No payment information needed. Choose from GPT-5, DeepSeek V3, Llama 4 and more.

Limitations: 15 requests per minute, 150 requests per day.

Why it matters: This is the lowest-barrier way to access GPT-5-class models for free.

OpenRouter — 28 Free Models

Register at openrouter.ai and search "free" — you'll find 28 complimentary models. One API key accesses the entire catalog.

Limitations: 50 requests/day without充值. Top up $10 for 1,000 requests/day (valid for one year).

Sambanova — $5 Free Credits

Register with a Google account and receive $5 in credits — roughly 20+ yuan. Browse compatible models in the Dashboard and create API keys from the left sidebar.

ZenMux — Enterprise-Grade Free Tier

A unified API platform with insurance-backed guarantees. Access OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, and DeepSeek models through a single endpoint. Imagen 2 and Agens 1.5 Pro are available for free.

Critical Tips: Don't Skip These

Enable "stop when depleted" immediately. Many platforms default to charging your card after free credits expire. Learn from my mistake.

Use expiring credits first. Zhipu's 3-month and Alibaba's 90-day credits should be consumed quickly. Save Tencent's 1-year credits for later.

Avoid hammering DeepSeek's official site. Their servers are frequently "busy." Get a key from Alibaba Bailian or SiliconFlow for a much smoother experience.

All free APIs have rate limits. Expect 1-50 requests per minute depending on the platform. Fine for testing and learning; production workloads require paid plans.

When to Upgrade to Paid

Free tiers are perfect for:

  • Learning and experimentation
  • Personal projects and prototypes
  • Small-scale automation scripts
  • Testing model capabilities before committing

Upgrade when you need:

  • Production-grade reliability
  • Higher rate limits
  • Longer context windows
  • Dedicated support

Free API credits are the tutorial gift box — they let you experience AI's power at zero cost. But what really anchors you in this field is picking your most annoying task, finding the right model, and plugging the key into your workflow. The first time you automate something that used to take 30 minutes, you'll know it was worth it.

❓ FAQ

Which platform gives the most free tokens?

Zhipu AI gives the most: 20 million tokens on registration, including GLM-4.6v and GLM-4.5-air, valid for 3 months.

Will free API credits charge my credit card?

Some platforms default to charging your card after credits run out. Make sure to enable the 'stop when depleted' setting.

Which platform should I use for DeepSeek?

Alibaba Bailian or SiliconFlow are recommended. Both support DeepSeek API calls without the 'server busy' issues of the official site.

How long do free API credits last?

Varies by platform: Tencent Cloud 1 year, Zhipu 3 months, Alibaba 90 days, Moonshot about 3 months.

Are there rate limits on free APIs?

Yes. Most platforms limit requests per minute. For high-frequency or production use, paid plans are recommended.

Which international platform is best?

GitHub Models offers free GPT-5, OpenRouter has 28 free models, and Sambanova gives $5 credit. Registering all three is worth it.
