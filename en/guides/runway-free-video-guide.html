Runway Free Tier 2026: 125 Credits for 25 Seconds of Video

Bottom line: Runway's free plan gives you 125 credits — one time, no monthly reset. That's roughly 25 seconds of Gen-4 Turbo video. If you want ongoing free AI video generation, Kling AI (66 credits/day) or Hailuo AI (100 credits/day) are far more generous. Runway's edge is cinematic quality and motion fluidity, but the free tier is basically a trial — not a real free plan.

What You Actually Get for Free

Runway's free plan includes more features than you'd expect, but the credit limit is the real bottleneck:

  • Video generation: Gen-4 Turbo (Image to Video) and Gen-3 Alpha Turbo — 125 credits ≈ 25 seconds total
  • Image generation: Gen-4 text-to-image and reference-based generation
  • AI chat: Built-in Gemini 3 Pro and Gemini 2 models (no credit cost)
  • Audio: Text-to-speech and AI audio generation
  • Image apps: 5 AI image editing tools
  • Video editor: 3 projects, 5GB storage

Critical limitations: No text-to-video on the free plan — only image-to-video. You need an existing image as a starting frame. Also, watermarks cannot be removed on the free tier. Every video you generate will have the Runway logo.

How Far Do 125 Credits Actually Go

Credit costs vary by model and duration:

  • Gen-4 Turbo (Image to Video): ~5 credits/second, so 125 credits ≈ 25 seconds
  • Gen-3 Alpha Turbo: ~5 credits/second, 125 credits ≈ 25 seconds
  • Gen-4 Text to Image: ~2-4 credits/image, 125 credits ≈ 30-60 images

These 125 credits are one-time only — they do not reset monthly. Once spent, they're gone. Compared to Kling AI (66 credits/day, resets daily), Runway's free plan is more of a "sample" than an actual free tier.

In practice: generating a 5-second clip costs about 25 credits. That means the entire free plan gives you roughly 5 clips at 5 seconds each. If you're iterating on prompts and testing different settings, you'll burn through the credits before getting 3 satisfactory results.

Free Tier Comparison: Runway vs Competitors

Here's how the major AI video tools stack up on free credits in 2026:

  • Kling AI: 66 credits/day (resets daily) — best for daily free use
  • Hailuo AI: 100 credits/day (resets daily) — most generous free tier
  • Runway: 125 credits (one-time) — best quality, worst free value
  • PixVerse: Daily free credits available
  • Luma Dream Machine: Free tier details require signup, paid plans from $30/month
  • Pika: Small daily free allocation

For long-term free video generation, Kling AI wins hands down. You get fresh credits every single day. Runway's 125 credits look generous on paper, but they're a one-shot deal. After that, you're looking at $12/month minimum.

Where Runway excels is raw output quality. Gen-4 Turbo produces videos with superior motion fluidity, lighting detail, and cinematic feel. But this advantage is hard to leverage on the free plan — 25 seconds isn't enough for any real project.

Is Standard Worth $12/Month?

The Standard plan is the cheapest paid option:

  • Price: $12/month (annual billing, $144/year)
  • Credits: 625 credits/month (resets monthly)
  • Equivalent: 625 credits ≈ 125 seconds of video/month (~2 minutes)
  • Unlocks: Text-to-video, all video models, watermark removal, higher resolution
  • Storage: 100GB

$12/month for 2 minutes of video is debatable value. Hailuo AI gives 100 credits/day for free. Kling AI gives 66 credits/day for free. Runway's paid plan costs more than competitors' free tiers. But if you specifically need cinematic-grade output (ads, short film素材, product showcases), Runway's quality justifies the price.

Getting Started: Registration and Tips

Signing up is straightforward:

  • Visit runway.com, register with Google or email
  • 125 credits are added automatically — no credit card required
  • Go to Dashboard → "Generative Video"
  • Upload a starting image for image-to-video
  • Write a motion description prompt, choose duration
  • Click Generate and wait

Pro tips to maximize your 125 free credits:

  • Test with text-to-image first: Image generation costs only 2-4 credits per image. Perfect your starting frame before spending 25 credits on a video generation attempt.
  • Stick to 5-second clips: 5 clips at 5 seconds gives you more iterations than 2 clips at 10 seconds.
  • Be specific with motion prompts: "Camera slowly pans right while the subject walks forward" works much better than vague terms like "cinematic" or "dynamic."

Hidden Free Features Most People Miss

Beyond video generation, Runway's free plan includes some underrated features:

  • Gemini 3 Pro chat: The built-in Gemini 3 Pro and Gemini 2 models work without consuming credits. Effectively a free Google Gemini interface.
  • Text-to-speech: Free TTS for voiceovers — no credit cost listed for basic usage.
  • Video editor: 3 project slots for basic cutting and splicing.
  • Image editing apps: 5 AI-powered tools including background removal and style transfer.

If you mainly need an AI chatbot, the Gemini 3 Pro access hidden inside Runway's free plan is actually a solid bonus — free access to Google's latest model without a separate Gemini subscription.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How many free credits does Runway give?
125 credits, one time only. They do not reset monthly. 125 credits equals about 25 seconds of Gen-4 Turbo video or 25 seconds of Gen-3 Alpha Turbo video.
Can I use Runway free for commercial projects?
Yes. Runway's free plan allows commercial use, but videos will have a Runway watermark. The free plan does not include watermark removal.
Is Runway free tier better than Kling AI?
Kling AI gives 66 credits daily (resets every day), making it better for long-term free use. Runway's 125 credits are one-time only. For quality, Runway excels at cinematic motion and film-like aesthetics, while Kling is better for general use.
What is Runway Gen-4 Turbo?
Gen-4 Turbo is Runway's latest video generation model supporting Image to Video. It improves on Gen-3 Alpha Turbo in motion consistency, visual quality, and generation speed. The free plan includes access to Gen-4 Turbo.
What happens when Runway free credits run out?
Free credits do not renew. Once used, you must upgrade to Standard ($12/month for 625 credits/month) or higher. Use the 125 free credits to test quality before committing to a paid plan.
