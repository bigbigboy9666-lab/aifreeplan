Manus uses a credit-based pricing model. Every action the agent takes — clicking a button, running code, reading a page — costs credits. You buy a monthly credit pool, and tasks draw from it.

How Manus AI Credits Work

)

Credits are consumed with every action Manus takes. The more complex the task, the more credits it burns. Here's a realistic breakdown:

10–50 credits: Simple questions, quick searches 100–300 credits: Web research across a few sources 200–500 credits: Code generation and execution 500–1,000 credits: Building a web application 500–900 credits: Deep research with cross-source analysis 800–1,500+ credits: Wide Research (parallel multi-source deep dive)

The catch: Manus doesn't tell you the credit cost before running a task. A "simple" research prompt that you expect to use 100 credits might burn 300–500 because the agent autonomously explores more pages than you anticipated.

Daily refresh vs. monthly credits: Daily refresh (300/day): Resets every 24 hours. Available on all plans including Free. Use it or lose it. Monthly credits: Your plan's allocation. Once depleted, you're back to daily refresh only until the next billing cycle.

Credit packs: Purchased add-on credits never expire as long as your subscription stays active — the only credits that roll over.

Is the Free Plan Enough?

The free plan gives you 300 daily credits and 1 concurrent task. Realistically, this means:

One moderate research task per day, OR Two to three simple queries per day Access to Manus 1.6 Lite only (reduced capability)

It's enough to test whether autonomous AI agents fit your workflow, but not enough for regular use. A single deep research task can consume your entire daily allocation.

Bottom line: Use the free plan to decide if Manus is worth paying for. Once you're convinced, the Standard plan at $20/month is the most cost-effective entry point.

What Can Manus AI Do? Five Core Use Cases

Deep Research: Feed Manus a research topic, and it autonomously browses dozens of sites, cross-references information, and delivers a structured report. This is where Manus dramatically outperforms traditional chatbots.

Web App Building: Manus can create functional web applications with databases, Stripe payment integration, and SEO optimization. Not as polished as dedicated tools, but solid for prototypes and MVPs.

Data Analysis: Upload a spreadsheet, and Manus automatically analyzes it, generates charts, and surfaces insights. Great for market research and competitive analysis.

AI Presentations: Describe a topic in one sentence, and Manus generates a complete slide deck. Useful for pitch decks, reports, and internal presentations.

Multi-Platform Access: Available via web, desktop app, Telegram, WhatsApp, and Slack. Delegate tasks from wherever you work.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Manus AI free? Yes, Manus offers a free plan at $0/month with 300 daily refresh credits and 1 concurrent task. No credit card required. However, you only get access to the Manus 1.6 Lite model.

How much does Manus AI cost per month? The Standard plan costs $20/month (4,000 credits), the Customizable plan costs $40/month (8,000 credits), and the Extended plan costs $200/month (40,000 credits). Annual billing saves 17% on all paid plans.

Do Manus AI credits roll over? Monthly credits do not roll over. Daily refresh credits reset every 24 hours. Only purchased add-on credit packs persist between months, as long as your subscription remains active.

Is Manus better than ChatGPT? It depends on your use case. Manus excels at autonomous multi-step tasks (web research, data analysis, app building) where you want the AI to work independently. ChatGPT is better for conversational use, writing, and quick Q&A. Both cost $20/month at the entry tier.

Does Manus AI have a free trial? The Customizable plan ($40/month) includes a 7-day free trial with full Pro access (8,000 credits, all three models). The $20 and $200 tiers do not have a separate trial.

Can Manus AI build mobile apps? No. Manus can build web applications but not native iOS or Android apps. For mobile development, look at FlutterFlow, React Native, or native development tools.

Is Manus owned by Meta? Meta announced a roughly $2 billion acquisition in December 2025, but China's antitrust regulator blocked the deal on April 27, 2026. Manus continues to operate independently, and the ownership status remains unresolved.

What's the cheapest way to try Manus AI? Start with the free plan (300 daily credits, $0/month). If you need more, the Standard plan at $20/month is the most affordable paid option. The Customizable plan's 7-day trial is also free if you want to test full Pro features.
