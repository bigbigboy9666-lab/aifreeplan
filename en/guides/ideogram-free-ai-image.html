Quick Summary

Ideogram delivers the most accurate text rendering of any AI image generator. The free tier allows approximately 25 images per day (based on community testing), with usable resolution and no watermark. Built by former Google Brain researchers, with version 3.0 released in February 2026.

What Is Ideogram

Ideogram is an AI image generation tool. It competes with Midjourney, DALL-E, and Stable Diffusion, but has one unique advantage: text rendering.

What does that mean? If you ask it to generate a poster that says "Happy Birthday," it actually gets the text right. Other AI image tools frequently produce garbled or distorted text.

The founding team includes former Google Brain researchers. Ideogram 3.0, released in February 2026, brought significant improvements to text rendering accuracy.

Core Capabilities

Text Rendering

)

Ideogram's signature feature. When you ask AI to write text on images, other tools often fail — Ideogram gets it right consistently.

Use cases:

  • Logo design (brand names)
  • Social media graphics (text-overlay images)

Comparison with Other AI Image Tools

Ideogram vs Midjourney vs DALL-E vs Stable Diffusion

  • Free tier available

Midjourney's advantages:

DALL-E's advantages:

  • Understands complex prompts

Stable Diffusion's advantages:

Real-World Use Cases

Poster Design: Tell Ideogram "Design a tech-themed poster with the title 'AI Conference 2026'" — it generates a poster with correctly rendered text.

Logo Design: Input your brand name and style description to generate logo concepts featuring your brand name.

Social Media Graphics: For example, "An illustration of a programmer coding in a coffee shop with the text 'Code & Coffee'."

Product Showcases: Combine product name + description to generate product display images.
