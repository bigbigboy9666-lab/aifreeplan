What is Ideogram

Ideogram is an AI image generation tool. It is a competitor to Midjourney, DALL-E, and Stable Diffusion, but with one unique advantage: text rendering.

What does that mean? If you ask it to generate a poster that says "Happy Birthday," it will actually render the text correctly. Other AI image tools often produce garbled or distorted text in images.

The founder is a former Google Brain researcher. In February 2026, Ideogram 3.0 was released, featuring significantly improved text rendering capabilities.
