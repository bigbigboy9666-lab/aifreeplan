DupDub Free AI Digital Human Guide: No Watermark, Chinese

DupDub is a free AI digital human video generation tool. No watermark, Chinese support. Best free D-ID alternative. This guide covers how to use it and best use cases.

What is DupDub?

  • Text to video: Input text, AI generates talking-head video
  • No watermark: Clean video output
  • Chinese support: Chinese voice and lip sync
  • Multiple faces: Choose from various digital human avatars
  • D-ID alternative: Similar features, larger free tier

How to Use

  1. Visit dupdub.com: Register
  2. Choose avatar: Select from preset digital humans
  3. Input text: Write your script

  5. Generate video: Wait a few minutes
