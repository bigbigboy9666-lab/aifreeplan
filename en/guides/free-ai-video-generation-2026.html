Recommendations for Different Needs

For short video material: Kling AI or Hailuo AI. Both offer daily free credits, with 5–10 second durations suitable for Douyin/Kuaishou content. Kling AI has better image quality; Hailuo AI offers more natural motion.

For social media posts: Pika. Rich in creative effects and fun style transformations, ideal for eye-catching short content.

For trying out AI video: Sora or Runway. Best image quality but limited free credits. Suitable for those curious about the current capabilities of AI video.

For professional projects: Runway’s paid version. Gen-3 Alpha’s image quality is the most reliable in professional scenarios.

For domestic (Chinese) users: Kling AI or Hailuo AI. No VPN needed, fast speed, and accurate understanding of Chinese prompts.

Pitfall Avoidance Guide — Hidden Limitations of Free Versions

Watermark issues: The free version of Kling AI includes a watermark; removing it requires payment. Some Hailuo AI videos may be watermark-free, but this is not guaranteed. Luma includes a watermark.

Duration limits: Free versions are generally capped at 5–10 seconds. For videos longer than 30 seconds, all tools require payment.

Resolution: Free versions max out at 1080p, with some limited to 720p. 4K output requires payment across all tools.

Commercial use restrictions: Videos generated on free versions are generally not licensed for commercial use. Commercial usage requires a paid subscription.

Queuing: Sora and Runway may have queues during peak times, with free users given the lowest priority.

FAQ

Which free AI video tool has the most credits in 2026?

Hailuo AI (MiniMax) offers 66-100 credits daily plus 300 signup bonus. Kling AI gives 66 credits/day. Both reset daily.

Can I use free AI-generated videos commercially?

No. All major AI video tools restrict commercial use on free tiers. A paid subscription is required for commercial rights.

Do free AI videos have watermarks?

Kling has watermarks (removable with paid plan). Hailuo sometimes has no watermark. Luma has watermarks. Runway and Pika free tiers include watermarks.

How long can free AI videos be?

Free tiers typically generate 5-10 second clips. Kling supports up to 10s, Hailuo 5-6s, Luma 5s. Longer videos require paid plans.

Which AI video tools work in China?

Kling AI and Hailuo AI are directly accessible in China. Runway, Pika, Sora, and Luma require VPN access.

Is there a big gap between free and paid versions?

Yes. Paid versions remove watermarks, offer higher resolution, longer durations, commercial rights, and priority queuing. Free tiers are best for trying out tools and personal projects.
