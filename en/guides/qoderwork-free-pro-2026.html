What Is QoderWork

QoderWork (formerly Alibaba Cloud's Lingma) is an AI desktop application by Alibaba Cloud. Officially positioned as a locally running, self-planning, secure and controllable AI work companion.

Simply tell it what you need in natural language, and it plans, executes, and delivers results. Unlike ChatGPT, QoderWork can directly operate your local files — generated PPTs, Excel sheets, PDFs are automatically saved to your computer without uploading/downloading.

❓ FAQ

Is QoderWork really free?

Yes. From June 1, download and log in to get 1 month PRO free, limited to 1 per person. Students and teachers can get resources equivalent to 3 months PRO.

Can QoderWork be used for coding?

Yes. QoderWork supports code generation, debugging, refactoring and other coding tasks, but it's positioned as a general AI office assistant, not just a coding tool.

How is QoderWork different from other AI coding tools?

QoderWork focuses more on general office scenarios, directly generating Word/Excel/PPT/PDF files with built-in browser automation. Cursor, Claude Code etc. focus more on coding.

What's the difference between PRO and free version?

PRO has more Credits quota, access to more powerful models, and full Expert Suites features. Free version also works but with limited quota.

What systems does QoderWork support?

Supports macOS 14+ and Windows 10+. Linux is not supported yet.
