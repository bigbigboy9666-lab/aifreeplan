Bing Image Creator Free Tier: The Details

Here's what you get for free:

  • 100 boosted generations per day (fast queue, 10-30 seconds)
  • Unlimited slow generations after 100 (longer wait times)
  • 1024x1024 resolution
  • No watermark
  • No credit card required
  • Personal use is free

"Boosted" means fast generation — usually 10-30 seconds. After you use your 100 daily boosts, images still generate but may take minutes instead of seconds. The boost counter resets daily.

There's no paid tier for Bing Image Creator itself. It's free. The only way to get more boosts is through a Microsoft 365 subscription.

How to Get Started

  1. Go to bing.com/images/create
  2. Sign in with a Microsoft account (free to create)
  3. Enter your prompt in English
  4. Click "Create" and wait 10-30 seconds
  5. Get 4 image variations to choose from

Important: DALL·E understands English much better than Chinese. Always write prompts in English for best results. "A cat sitting on a windowsill watching the sunset, watercolor style" produces far better results than the Chinese equivalent.

Bing Image Creator vs Other Free AI Image Tools

Here's how it compares:

Ideogram: 25 free/day, best text rendering in images Leonardo AI: 15 free/day, more control options Playground AI: 500 free/day, but lower quality Adobe Firefly: 25 credits/month, Adobe ecosystem integration Midjourney: No free tier, starts at $10/month

Bing Image Creator's advantage: DALL·E quality, completely free, no VPN needed. Its weakness: less style control than specialized tools, queue times can be long during peak hours.

Prompt Tips for Better Results

The quality of your prompt directly affects output. Here's what works:

Add style keywords: Append "oil painting", "watercolor", "digital art", "photorealistic", "anime style" to your description.

Specify composition: Add "close-up shot", "wide angle", "bird's eye view", "cinematic lighting".

Be detailed: "A red sports car on a rainy Tokyo street at night, neon reflections on wet pavement" beats "a red car" by a mile.

Avoid abstract concepts: DALL·E struggles with abstract ideas. The more concrete and visual your description, the better.

Use negative prompts in descriptions: Instead of "a cat", write "a fluffy orange tabby cat sitting on a blue velvet cushion".

Common Questions

❓ FAQ

Is Bing Image Creator completely free?

Yes. 100 boosted generations per day, unlimited slow generations after that. No credit card required.

What model does Bing Image Creator use?

It uses OpenAI's DALL·E model. This is the only free official way to use DALL·E.

Does Bing Image Creator support Chinese prompts?

Technically yes, but English prompts produce much better results with DALL·E.

Can I use Bing Image Creator images commercially?

The free plan is for personal use only. Commercial use requires additional licensing.

Bing Image Creator vs Ideogram: which is better?

Bing offers 100 free/day with DALL·E quality. Ideogram offers 25 free/day but excels at text rendering in images.

Do I need a VPN for Bing Image Creator?

Depends on your region. Most areas can access it directly with a Microsoft account.
