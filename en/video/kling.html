Kling AI

★★★★☆ 66 credits/day Paid from: $8/month

Visit Official Site →

Kling AI is Kuaishou's flagship video generation model. It produces some of the most realistic AI videos available today — smooth motion, consistent characters, and solid 1080p output. You get 66 free credits daily, enough for roughly 3-6 short clips per day.

Kling AI Free Credits Details

  • Amount: 66 credits
  • Reset: Resets daily
  • Watermark: Yes
  • Commercial: Paid only

Kling AI Key Features

  • Text to Video
  • Image to Video
  • Up to 1080p
  • Motion Brush
  • Lip Sync

Pros

  • 66 free credits every day — no payment required
  • Produces 5-10 second clips at up to 1080p resolution
  • Image-to-video mode generates more realistic results than text-only
  • Strong Chinese language prompt understanding
  • No VPN needed for users in China

Cons

  • Free clips include a watermark (remove with paid plan)
  • Maximum clip length is 10 seconds on the free tier
  • Complex camera movements sometimes produce artifacts
  • Commercial use requires a paid subscription

Tips

  • Use image-to-video instead of text-only — the quality jump is significant
  • Be specific in your prompts: describe camera angle, motion, and scene details
  • At $8/month, the paid plan removes watermarks and unlocks longer clips
  • Compare with Hailuo AI (free, no watermark) if watermarks are a dealbreaker
